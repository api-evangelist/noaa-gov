This article explores potential impacts of El Niño on West Coast marine species, examining how warming Pacific waters could affect fisheries, marine ecosystems, and coastal communities. It discusses shifts in species distribution, changes in fish migration patterns, and ecological consequences of elevated ocean temperatures during El Niño events.
